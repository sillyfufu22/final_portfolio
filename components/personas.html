Washing Machines @ Brown Dorms

A Personas & Storyboarding Project

Context

In this project, I am creating personas based on how Brown University students are interacting with dormitory washing machines, interviewing them on their experiences, and illustrating a storyboard for one of these personas. The reason I chose washing machines as my interface to learn more about user experiences is because when I came to Brown as a freshman, I struggled to understand all the functionalities and purposes of the washing machine interface and wanted to use this as an opportunity to improve on the designs of dormitory washing machines which the whole Brown community interacts with.

Research

I started off this project by learning more about the different types of washing machines at Brown and preparing questions for students regarding their experience with the washing machines. Questions included:

  1. How often do you do your laundry?
    1. More than once a week
    2. Once a week
    3. Once every 2 weeks
    4. No set schedule / whenever I need to
    5. Other

  2. How many loads do you run when you do your laundry?
    1. 1
    2. 2
    3. 3
    4. Other

  3. How much clothes do you put in your washer
    1. Up to the brim
    2. 3/4
    3. 1/2
    4. Less than 1/2

  4. Where do you put your tide pods?
    1. In the dispenser drawer
    2. In the washer drum, on top of clothes
    3. In the washer drum, on the bottom of clothes

  5. I understand the following options and their purposes:
    1. Water Temperature
    2. Soil Level
    3. Spin Level
    4. Time

  6. What does your schedule/day look like when you do your laundry? What do you plan to do while your clothers are in the washer?

  7. After I put my clothes in the washer, I
    1. set a timer and pick them up right away when they are done
    2. set a timer but sometimes I will not pick them up right away
    3. don't set a timer and pick them up when I remember or when it is convienent for me to pick them up
    4. Other: describe

Then I recorded students' responses and summarized them. The two personas that I created based on these responses are: Meticulous Marty and Confused Freshman.

Design Iterations

In my webpage, I included sketches of the washing machine with annotations of key interactive components so that readers have more clarity of the interface. I also included a storyboard to better represent the persona of the users.

Conclusion

This project is the first project for my UI UX class so even though it was just an HTML file, there was a lot of trial and error. Nevertheless, I learned a great deal from this experience and the comments from the UX factor were helpful for my future projects.
